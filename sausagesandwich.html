Super Easy Slow Cooker Sausage Sandwich

This easy recipe requires just a few ingredients and minimal prep time.

Just add everything to the crock pot and cook for four hours!

Ingredients

  • One package of Italian Sausages
  • One jar of marinara sauce
  • One large bell pepper
  • One large onion
  • One tablespoon of brown sugar
  • Hoagie rolls
  • Provolone Cheese

Steps

  1. Cut the pepper and onion into strips.
  2. Add the marinara sauce and brown sugar to slow cooker and mix well.
  3. Add the sausages and cut vegetables to the slow cooker and cover with sauce.
  4. Cook on low for eight hours or on high for four hours.
  5. Add sausage to a roll with a slice of provolone cheese and cover with sauce and veggies.

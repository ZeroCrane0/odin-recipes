Slow Cooker Chicken Tacos

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 jar of salsa
  • 1 packet of taco seasoning
  • Tortillas
  • Toppings

Steps

  1. Add chicken breasts, salsa, and taco seasoning to slow cooker.
  2. Cook on low for eight hours or on high for four hours.
  3. Add to tortillas with desired toppings
